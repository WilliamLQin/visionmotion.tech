Privacy Policy

Summary

Vision Motion requests access to your camera data in order to function properly. It uses the camera to track the motion of an object.

The app uses Google Play Services to enable the log in service and Firebase database. If you choose the use the cloud data saving service by logging into your account, the app will record the position data created through the use of the app to a Firebase database. This is to be able to give it back to you later.

The app also requests access to your storage to write files in the case you would like to download your data.
